Lens PMC is a prototype under development.

We hope to continue to build out this sandbox with a wide set of scholarly communication tools. Areas of interest include but are not limited to:

  • discovery (search, visualization-based interactivity, article recommendations)
  • semantic enrichment
  • annotation, etc.

If you are interested in contributing to the development of the Lens PMC prototype, please contact Ivan Grubisic or Jennifer Lin. We always welcome general feedback as well.
